🌈 About Our Amazing School

Founded in 1995, Sunrise Primary School has been a magical place where young minds bloom and dreams come true! 🌸 Our mission is to nurture curiosity, creativity, and compassion in every precious child. Through innovative teaching methods, vibrant community involvement, and a commitment to holistic growth, we've created a wonderful learning environment where children love to explore, discover, and grow! 🌟

🎨 Our Fun-Filled Curriculum

📚

Literacy & Language

Exciting reading adventures, creative storytelling, and strong communication skills in English and other languages through interactive games and fun activities!

🔢

Mathematics

Hands-on learning of numbers, problem-solving, and logical thinking through engaging games, puzzles, and real-world challenges that make math magical!

🎭

Arts & Creativity

Colorful drawing, painting, music, drama, and crafts that inspire imagination, self-expression, and artistic talents in every child!

⚽

Physical Education

Fun sports, yoga, dance, and outdoor activities to build fitness, teamwork, confidence, and healthy habits for life!
